The updates are overwhelming. I get it. But after testing **Gemini 3.0** for a month, I've ignored the hype and found the 5 features that will actually change how you work.

## 1. True Multimodality (No More Screenshots)
Gemini 3 doesn't just "see" frames; it understands video and audio together.
**The Play:** Upload a messy screen recording of a workflow -> Ask Gemini to turn it into a clean SOP checklist for new hires. It watches the video, listens to your voiceover, and nails the steps.

## 2. Active Working Memory (The Conflict Detector)
Stop treating the context window like a storage bin. Gemini 3 is 60% better at digging deep into large files.
**The Play:** Upload your company's "Strategic Goals" (Video Call) and your "Financial Reports" (PDFs). Ask: *"Where do these contradict each other?"* It will find the lies.

**The Play:** "Find every email from [Client Name] about 'Deadlines' and draft a summary of what I owe them." It scans Gmail and Drive accurately.

## 5. Context Engineering (RIP Prompt Engineering)
Stop obsessing over adjectives ("Make it punchy!"). Start giving context.
**The Play:** Instead of describing your tone, upload 3 of your best LinkedIn posts and say: *"Rewrite this report to sound like these."* It clones your vibe perfectly.
